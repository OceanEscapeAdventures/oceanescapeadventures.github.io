Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the price?

Everything listed in your package: certified captain, fuel, life jackets and equipment. No additional fees — optional add-ons like BBQ or water sports are priced clearly.

How many guests can join?

Up to 7 guests per trip. While you're on board, the boat is exclusively yours — we never mix groups. Only on the Boat Pick-Up & Drop package, the boat doesn't wait with you after drop-off: it returns to Marasy or serves other guests until you call for your return pick-up.

What should I bring?

Swimwear, a towel, sunscreen, sunglasses and a hat. We provide water, ice and all safety equipment.

Can children come along?

Absolutely — families are very welcome. Life jackets are provided for all ages, and our captain keeps the ride smooth and safe.

What happens if the weather is bad?

Your safety comes first. If sea conditions aren't suitable, we'll contact you to reschedule your trip to the next convenient date.

How do I book and pay?

Choose your package and tap Book via WhatsApp — we'll confirm your date, time and details in minutes and share payment options with you directly. Please note all payments are made in AED (UAE dirham); the USD figures shown are approximate references only — your bank or card issuer sets the final conversion rate and any fee.
